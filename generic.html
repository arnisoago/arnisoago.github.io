Jack Agolli

I'm an aerospace engineer soon to complete my undergraduate degree at WPI, and am passionate about advancing space exploration and space flight technologies.

About Me

I have been driven by a desire to advance space exploration since as early as elementary school. Then, when I was in middle school, my family and I visited Kennedy Space Center. It was my first actual exposure to the space industry and NASA and I was able to see models of the Space Shuttle and the Saturn V firsthand. I purchased a small textbook on our solar system there and studied it for hours on end (I still have it to this day). I have since shaped my career so that I can help advance space exploration and our understanding of the universe.

Last summer I interned at NASA Goddard Space Flight Center, which is largest collection of scientists and engineers in the world.
